## 📖 About iNook

**iNook** has a design that is true to the game and has a lot of features built in that other game-related apps don't have. You can easily access information related to **Animal Crossing New Horizons**, and keep complete track of your museum, your catalog or your closet.  
  
Learn about all the recipes and projects available in the game, discover the construction elements in the public works, including the different types of fences available for decorating.  
  
You can share your profile with other players who have the app from the passport to add you as a friend so they can easily view your friend or dream code and visit your islands.  
  
Discover the mysterious islands you can travel to with all sorts of details, including which ones are available and which ones are not. You will also be able to visualize the different cards available to send, or the variations of gyroids that are available with their type of sound.  
  
You will find all the products of the game in the catalog where you will find all the objects, both furniture and clothing, with all the details and variations available in the game.  
  
Achieve the objectives and get your miles to get objects in the game, since from iNook you will be able to visualize and mark all the objectives you have fulfilled, besides being able to visualize all the possible houses to design in Happy Home Paradise.  
  
View content from creators with the video section from which you can access content from various YouTubers and learn details, tricks and curiosities of the game.  
  
All this and much more awaits you inside the app, in which we hope to offer new features in future updates to make your gaming experience much greater and more complete.  
  
For details, improvements, suggestions or anything else, do not hesitate to contact us on Twitter, our Discord or by email: helpmejeluchu@gmail.com

## ❓ FAQ

**Is the app free? Does it have ads?**

Yes, iNook is completely free and has no ads. If you'd like to support the project's development, you can do so via [PayPal](https://www.paypal.com/paypalme/jeluchu), [Ko-Fi](https://ko-fi.com/jeluchu), or you can also support us by subscribing to the subscription that has been created within the application, which will give you extra customisation options within the app.

**Is the game data up to date?**

Yes. Data is updated remotely, meaning new items and content are added without needing to update the app. App updates bring improvements, new features, and bug fixes.

**What if I don't have the Play Store?**

If you have a Huawei device without access to the Play Store, you can download iNook from the [App Gallery](https://appgallery.huawei.com/app/C105452903).

**Can I export or import my data?**

iNook includes a local backup system. You can generate a backup file and restore it when you switch phones or reinstall the app. Cloud sync is not currently available.

**Can I register more than one island?**

No, the app currently only supports registering one island and one islander per device.

**Does it link directly to Nintendo Switch?**

No. Third-party apps cannot access Nintendo Switch game data directly. Only Nintendo's official apps can link to your Switch account and read your game data.
